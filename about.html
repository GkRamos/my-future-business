About Us

In this page you will now the history and background about our shop

5 Sept 2016

Background

Established on September 5, 2018, Gie's Cycling Shop is not just a store; it's a passion-driven hub for all cycling enthusiasts. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a space where the love for cycling, quality products, and exceptional service converge.

From the inception, Gie's Cycling Shop was founded on the belief that cycling is more than just a sport; it's a lifestyle. We aimed to foster a community where cyclists of all levels could find the right gear, receive expert advice, and share their love for the open road or challenging trails.

In the early days, our humble shop started as a small storefront with a curated selection of bikes and accessories. Our founders, avid cyclists themselves, poured their expertise and passion into every aspect of the business. The goal was simple — to be a one-stop destination where cyclists could find everything they needed to enhance their riding experience.

27 July 2018

Growth and Expansion

As the cycling community embraced Gie's Cycling Shop, we quickly outgrew our initial space. The demand for quality bikes, top-notch accessories, and expert advice drove us to expand our product range and services. We moved to a larger location to accommodate the growing needs of our customers and to provide an even more immersive cycling shopping experience.

Beyond being a retail space, Gie's Cycling Shop actively engages with the local cycling community. We organize group rides, workshops, and events to bring cyclists together, fostering a sense of camaraderie and shared passion. Our commitment to community extends beyond our store walls, and we actively support local cycling initiatives and environmental sustainability efforts.

11 Oct 2020

Team and Looking Forward

At the heart of Gie's Cycling Shop is our dedicated team of cycling enthusiasts. Our knowledgeable staff comprises seasoned cyclists who understand the diverse needs of riders. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned pro, our team is here to offer personalized advice, ensuring you find the perfect bike and accessories for your style and preferences.

As we reflect on our journey so far, we are grateful for the support of our loyal customers and the vibrant cycling community. Looking forward, Gie's Cycling Shop is committed to continuous improvement, innovation, and providing an unparalleled experience for cyclists who choose us as their trusted cycling partner. Join us on this exciting ride as we continue to evolve, inspire, and contribute to the ever-growing cycling culture. Gie's Cycling Shop — Where Your Cycling Journey Begins!
